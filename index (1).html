Obito Uchiha

Obito Uchiha (うちはオビト, Uchiha Obito) was a member of Konohagakure's Uchiha clan. He was believed to have died during the Third Shinobi World War

Madara Uchiha

Madara Uchiha (うちはマダラ, Uchiha Madara) was the legendary leader of the Uchiha Clan.

Itachi Uchiha

Itachi Uchiha (うちはイタチ, Uchiha Itachi) was a shinobi of Konohagakure's Uchiha clan who served as an Anbu Captain. He later became an international criminal after murdering his entire clan
